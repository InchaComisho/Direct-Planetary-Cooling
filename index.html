Core Position

Direct Planetary Cooling is presented as a personal hypothesis and precautionary proposal, not as an absolute prediction. It does not replace emissions reduction. It argues that emissions reduction should be combined with carbon sink restoration, soil and microbial regeneration, ocean and water-cycle restoration, ecosystem recovery, and nature-compatible cooling support.

地球直接冷却は、絶対的な予測ではなく、個人仮説であり予防原則に基づく提案です。排出削減の代替ではなく、炭素固定源再生、土壌・微生物循環、海洋・水循環、生態系回復、自然補完型冷却を組み合わせる構想です。
