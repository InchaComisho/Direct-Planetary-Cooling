# Cooling Credits as the Implementation Layer of Direct Planetary Cooling

[← Back to README](README.md)

---

## Overview

Direct Planetary Cooling is the higher-level concept for restoring and restarting the natural cooling functions that Earth originally possessed.

However, even if the direction of the solution is visible, humanity does not move fast enough through goodwill or morality alone.

Restoring forests, water cycles, soils, ocean circulation, microbes, humus, and urban heat balance is necessary.

But without a profit structure, society will not implement these actions at sufficient speed and scale.

Therefore, Direct Planetary Cooling needs an institutional, investment, and business implementation layer.

That layer is the Cooling Credit Framework.

---

## Basic Relationship

```text
Direct Planetary Cooling
= The higher-level concept for restoring Earth’s original natural cooling functions

Cooling Credits
= The incentive system that assigns economic value to those restoration actions
```

Direct Planetary Cooling explains what must be restored.

Cooling Credits explain how society can be made to implement it.

---

## Why Cooling Credits Are Necessary

The essence of climate action is to restore weakened natural phenomena of the Earth.

But human society does not move on correctness alone.

It requires:

```text
profit
investment
business models
institutional design
maintenance contracts
regional economies
international finance
```

Cooling Credits are designed to redirect these forces toward natural regeneration.

> Cooling Credits are not a moral argument for protecting nature. They are a system for replacing the engine of natural destruction with an engine of natural regeneration.

---

## Connection as a Market Thesis

Direct Planetary Cooling explains how natural cooling functions can be restored.

However, explanation alone does not produce implementation at scale.

The Cooling Credit Market Thesis turns planetary cooling actions into a large climate-adaptation market, giving companies, investors, municipalities, insurers, infrastructure operators, agriculture, fisheries, and tourism sectors a reason to participate.

```text
Desire and profit
↓
Entry into the Cooling Credit market
↓
Expansion of cooling and regeneration businesses
↓
Local and regional heat-load reduction
↓
Support for water cycles, evapotranspiration, clouds, rainfall, and ocean circulation
↓
Recovery of natural cooling functions
```

Awareness reform is not the starting condition.

After measurable results appear, society can finally recognize that restoring natural phenomena is itself a climate solution.

---

## Natural Cooling Functions to Be Valued

Cooling Credits should not value cooling devices alone.

They should value restoration of natural cooling functions such as:

```text
rain
clouds
evaporation
evapotranspiration
water circulation
soil water retention
forest cooling
microbial circulation
humus formation
ocean vertical circulation
phytoplankton carbon fixation
urban waste-heat reduction
```

Restoring these functions is the implementation of Direct Planetary Cooling, and the Cooling Credit Framework provides the incentive layer for that implementation.
