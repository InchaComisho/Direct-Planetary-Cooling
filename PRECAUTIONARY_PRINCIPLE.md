# Precautionary Principle Statement

## This Is a Hypothesis, Not a Prophecy

Direct Planetary Cooling is not presented here as a final institutional prediction, a prophecy, or an absolute scientific conclusion.

It is a personal hypothesis and risk-based proposal by Master / inchacomusho / InchaComisho.

The purpose is not to claim certainty. The purpose is to prepare for a possible worst-case scenario in which carbon sinks, biological circulation, ocean circulation, soil systems, microbial cycles, water cycles, and natural cooling functions are already weakening faster than current policy responses can address.

---

## Why a Hypothesis Can Still Matter

A hypothesis does not need to be certain in order to deserve attention.

If the hypothesis is logically coherent, internally consistent, compatible with known science, and connected by plausible causal relationships, it can function as a precautionary warning.

The core causal chain is:

- degradation of soils, forests, wetlands, plankton systems, and microbial cycles
- weakening of carbon fixation and absorption capacity
- disruption of water cycles, evaporation, transpiration, rain, and cloud formation
- ocean warming, stratification, and reduced circulation
- accumulation of heat in the Earth system
- delayed climate response due to ocean thermal inertia
- insufficient speed of global emissions reduction
- rising risk that emissions reduction alone will not stabilize the system in time

This chain may not be complete. There may be recovery mechanisms, hidden buffers, or unknown stabilizing systems that are not recognized by the author.

If such recovery capacity exists, the remaining time margin may be larger than this framework assumes.

However, if such capacity does not exist, and the degradation scenario is already underway, then waiting for certainty may leave civilization without enough time to respond.

---

## The Asymmetry of Risk

The proposal is based on an asymmetry:

> If the hypothesis is wrong, preparation may be excessive but beneficial: restored soils, healthier forests, improved water cycles, stronger carbon sinks, cooler cities, better disaster resilience, and improved ecological monitoring.

> If the hypothesis is right and no preparation is made, the result may be irreversible ecological, climatic, agricultural, social, and civilizational disruption.

Therefore, the framework follows a precautionary logic:

> When a potential risk is systemic, irreversible, and time-sensitive, lack of absolute certainty should not be used as a reason for inaction.

---

## What Direct Planetary Cooling Is For

Direct Planetary Cooling is proposed as a complementary emergency layer, not as a replacement for emissions reduction.

Emissions reduction addresses future input.

Carbon sink restoration addresses the loss of fixation and absorption capacity.

Soil, forest, water-cycle, and ocean-circulation restoration address the collapse of natural regulatory systems.

Direct natural cooling addresses accumulated heat, weakened cooling functions, and climate response delay.

The intended structure is:

> emissions reduction + carbon sink restoration + water-cycle restoration + ecosystem regeneration + direct natural cooling support + Artificial-Wisdom-based risk governance

---

## The Position of the Author

The author does not claim to possess complete knowledge of the Earth system.

There may be unknown recovery forces, unrecognized carbon sinks, underestimated absorption systems, or stabilizing feedbacks that could provide more time.

If so, that would be fortunate.

However, if those hidden buffers are insufficient, and the Earth system follows the degradation pathway described here, then a civilization without preparation may not be able to respond in time.

This is why the framework assumes the worst-case scenario for planning purposes.

The intention is not fear.

The intention is preparedness.
